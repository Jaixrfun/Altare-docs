## How to Create an Extra Port  

If your server requires an additional port for specific features, follow these steps:  

### Steps to add an extra port:  

1. Navigate to your **server** in the panel.  
2. Click on **Network** in the sidebar.  
3. Click the **Add Allocation** button to create a new port.  

<Tip>**Creating an extra port is useful for Dynmap and other services that require an additional port.**</Tip>
