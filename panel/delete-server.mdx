## How to Delete a Server  

If you no longer need your server, you can delete it directly from the panel.  

### Steps to delete your server:  

1. Navigate to your **server** in the panel.  
2. Click on **Settings** in the sidebar.  
3. Scroll down to find the **Delete Server** button.  
4. Click the button and confirm the action when prompted.  

<Warning>**This will permanently delete your server and all its data. Your allocated resources will be returned.**</Warning>  

Be sure to back up any important data before proceeding, as this action **cannot** be undone.
